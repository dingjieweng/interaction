Hello!

Danny is a digital product designer who grew up in Taipei, Taiwan, and currently resides in New York City. He is a designer who loves crafting beautiful, user-centered products through user testing and research.Prior to starting at SVA's Interaction Design program, he worked as a brand designer for a Hong Kong-based tech startup for nearly two years. During that time, he worked with teams across the region to develop functional design methodology and grew his passion for creating valuable and intuitive products for users.Aside from work, Danny is a movie lover, plant caretaker, and occasional swimmer. He also loves exploring new cultures and trying new services of technology.

My Career so far

I started my career as a brand designer in Snapask, a Hong Kong based startup company provides online services for both students and tutors.
