Project Overview
Acid Reﬂux, or so called Gastroesophageal Reﬂux Disease(GERD) is a common disease, about 20 percent of people in the United States have GERD. The project aims to create a solution that help people solve/alleviate the pain that people are suffering from GERD.

Prototype
How might we help people who suffer from acid reflux rebuild their eating habit?
Problem Statement
Find your local meal provider,
Book meal plans in advance.
Through eating time, you get access to local meal providers. Book your meal plans with your favorite food and start build your eating habit.
Re-build your eating routine with us
Through our meal reminder, you could use the preset to start to rebuild your eating habit, and choose the frequency to start. You could upload photos every meal to keep on track and stay informed. And we'll also keep this record for you.
Through news report, I already knew that people with GERD might have cause, sometimes multiple factors. To dive deeper and validate my assumptions, I conduct interviews with 4 people had GERD.

Key findings

1. People do have the same problems : irregular lifestyle, work overtime, etc. Besides that, they seldom cook themselves, they may eat in the diner, order takeouts or delivery.
2. Due to high pressure working life, when they choose food to eat, they tend to choose unhealthy food, cause eating those is reliving.
3. Even though the reasons causing GERD are multiple, every interviewees talks about their eating habits and disorder affect them.

User Persona
To understand more about our user, I mapped out the user journey. The journey map sums up the overall experience from start to the end of people suffer from GERD. I choose to focus on helping people on recognizing and treating stage because that's when people started to seeking for help.
User Journey Map
Through my research, I found out that the cause of GERD has multiple reasons. The reasons  varied based on different people. But the phase of irregular eating habit, not eat on time is the common ground they all have. I choose to focus on helping user rebuild their eating habit because majority of user are suffering from this.
My Approach
I built the flow chart imagined how people would  achieve their goal using my design.
Flow chart
Built lo-fi prototype and test it with user to validate my idea. I asked user to achieve goal of using meal reminder and order a meal plan. Through the process, I asked them to share out loud of what they are thinking.
Prototype & Usability Test

What could be improved

1. User find overall the flow is too long. Both meal reminder and ordering food.
2. User is confused about the part that building a routine and choose a meal from a picture. When choosing a meal from picture, user directly thought it as ordering a meal for today. User doesn't think it as a long term thing for building up a habit.
3. User would love to know more about the food provider, who is cooking the food? where is it from?

Direction of refinement

1. Shorten the user flow to help them achieve their goal
2. Building pre-set options for them to use more intuitively
3. Adding up more details about food provider

What went well

1. User is able to begin with meal reminder and find it helpful.
2. User is able to choose meals and continue the process.
Findings
Iteration progress
I iterated my design to better serve user needs. Shorten the user flow to help them achieve their goal, and have pre-set options for them to easier to go through the process.
